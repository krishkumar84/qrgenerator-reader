How do I scan a QR code?

To scan a QR code, Scroll above to QR code reader and click on that and upload QR image The app will then read the code and display the information encoded within it.

How do I create a QR code?

Scroll above to QR code generator shown that allow you to create a QR code for free. Simply enter the text you want to encode, and the tool will generate a QR code for you to download and also share to use.

Can QR codes be customized?

Yes, QR codes can be customized with colors, and in different sizes to make them more visually appealing.
